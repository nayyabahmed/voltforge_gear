# Cost Ledger

What the project has actually cost, purchase by purchase. The
[BOM](BOM.md) says what the buggy *needs*; this ledger records what was
*spent*, so the two can be compared honestly.

Guiding principle 1 applies: **learn more than you spend.** Guiding
principle 3 applies too: **spend progressively** - each purchase should
unlock the next useful experiment.

## Maintenance Rules

1. Record every purchase within a week, while the reasoning is fresh.
2. Update the cumulative total each time.
3. A failed purchase is a required entry, not an embarrassment.
4. Review the ledger at each stage boundary (Part 4 chapters) before the
   next round of spending.
